• 資料保存在裝置上——不需帳號、不需網路也能管理書庫

適合誰？

• 二手書賣家、書店與擺攤書商——掃描收書、管理庫存、掌握利潤
• 藏書愛好者——快速為整櫃書建檔
• 學生與老師——整理班級書單與個人書庫

現在就掃第一本書，開始打造你的書庫。

# English (U.S.)

## App Name (≤30 chars)
ISBN Book Manager

## Subtitle (≤30 chars)
Scan, Catalog & Track Profit

## Promotional Text (≤170 chars)
Scan a barcode, get the book cataloged instantly. Track buy price, sell price and profit — built for book lovers and resellers alike.

## Description
Point your camera at a book's barcode and it's cataloged instantly — title, author and cover filled in automatically. More than a library organizer: it keeps your books' money side in order too, tracking what you paid, what you sold for, and your profit on every book.

Key Features:

• Fast barcode scanning — point at the ISBN barcode and the lookup is done
• Automatic book info — title, author and cover auto-filled; manual entry and title search also available
• Buy & sell price tracking — record purchase and sale price per book
• Automatic profit calculation — per-book and total profit at a glance
• Sales statistics — inventory, sold items and efficiency reports
• Your data stays on your device — no account needed, works offline

Who is it for?

• Used-book sellers and booksellers — scan incoming stock, manage inventory, know your margins
• Book collectors — catalog an entire shelf in minutes
• Students and teachers — organize class reading lists and personal libraries

Scan your first book and start building your library today.

# App Store Connect 手動操作清單

1. **分類**：App Information → Primary Category 由「Utilities 工具程式」改為「Books 圖書」（競品聚集處，Apple 會自動把分類名納入搜尋索引，「圖書」不必佔關鍵字欄）。
2. **名稱/副標題/關鍵字**：隨下一版 binary 送審才能改（keywords 欄逐字貼上，逗號後無空格）。
3. **Promotional Text**：不需送審，可立即更新。
4. **價格**：改 freemium 時將 app 價格調為「免費」，同步上架 IAP（見 IAP 任務）。
